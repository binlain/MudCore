Lua API(zmq):

- constants: mud.zmq.CONTANT_NAME <=> ZMQ_CONSTANT_NAME
- s:watch(in=func(s), out=func(s))
- s:setopt(opt, val)
- s:getopt(opt)
- s:bind(addr)
- s:connect(addr)
- s:send(str)
- s:send(str,flags)
- s:recv()
- s:recv(flags)

return values, where an error can happen are status, msg. for s:recv,
return msg, errmsg. errmsg is non-nil iff msg is nil.
